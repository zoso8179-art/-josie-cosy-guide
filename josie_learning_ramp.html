The order matters. Email and Klaviyo first — always. That's the core of the role and the thing Cosy will judge the 90 days on. Analytics second, because you can't improve what you can't measure. SEO third, because product copy is a quick win you can deliver early. PPC fourth — support role, not ownership. Social alongside everything, because it's lower stakes and you can learn by doing. Don't try to learn all of this at once. Learn it in the order it appears here, and the knowledge will compound.
